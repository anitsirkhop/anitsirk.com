Kristina Hopkins

I'm Kristina. Buffalo, New York born. Lover of all things design. Passionate about digital publishing and letterpress printing. A sucker for ball terminal and slab serif typefaces. Longing to live where it snows too much in the winter and at the beach the rest of the year (I know...it's a little backwards!) I live in York, Pennsylvania, never far away from my iPad chock-full of digital magazine, an ever-growing library of graphic design books and magazines.

I've been a graphic designer since 2010 and have ganed valuable experience working in design and communications environments within associations, family-owned businesses and Fortune 500 corporations. I currently specialize in the design of print and digital publications and sales marketing materials and have an interest in working in branding and gaining more experience in web design & front-end web development.
